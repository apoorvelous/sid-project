About Us

Our aim is to provide our clients with a personalised and customised design that goes beyond their imagination. We are basically reshaping the concept of luxury with sustainability & making our clients to fall in love with “India’s environment friendly interior design” all over again. We choose to focus our work mainly on trying to translate “extravagant fantasies into well designed functional spaces that anthropomorphise grandeur, richness and unpolluting.” As we have a greater clarity which allows the architectural ideas to be understood most clearly- the difference between opacity and transparency, solid and void, structure and surface because we believe in words of late RENZO MONGIARDINO (one of the biggest inﬂuencers) :- “I want to create the illusion-and the reality-of permanence.
